Brown Sugar Chicken



Description

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 6 chicken tenders
  • 2 tbsps. flour
  • 1 tsp. EACH paprika, garlic powder, onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp. EACH> chipotle chili pepper, ground cumin, salt, black pepper
  • 2 tbsps. butter
  • 2 tbsps. olive oil
  • White vinegar for cleaning the chicken

Brown Sugar Sauce

  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp. EACH (dried) parsley, oregano, thyme.
  • Red pepper flakes
